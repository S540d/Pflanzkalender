Planting Calendar is your clear companion for the whole gardening year – from the first sowing to the last harvest.

The calendar works with a half-month resolution (24 periods per year), so you can see at a glance what needs doing in the bed, the raised bed or on the balcony.

🌱 WHAT YOU GET
• 30+ ready-made plants with typical activities
• Clear plant icons – recognisable at a glance, no reading required
• Activities such as sowing, planting, fertilising, pruning, protecting and harvesting
• Coloured time bars right in the calendar – just drag & drop to move them
• Add your own plants and adjust the timing to your climate

📅 ALWAYS IN CONTROL
• Calendar view across the whole year
• Agenda shows what is due this week and the weeks ahead
• Filter by category: vegetables & herbs, flowers, trees
• Location hints: sun, partial shade, shade
• Climate recommendations for hot and dry spots

🔄 SHARE & BACK UP
• Export and import your planting plans as a file
• Community templates for a quick start (e.g. Balcony Starter)
• Share templates easily via QR code
• All data stays local on your device

✨ COMFORT
• Light and dark mode
• Multilingual (including English, German, Spanish)
• Works offline – no account needed

Planting Calendar is free, ad-free and does not collect personal data.

Happy gardening! 🌻
